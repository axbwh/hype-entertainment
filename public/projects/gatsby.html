Ernst & Young

Thank you for everything. The venue looked amazing. It was a great night and everyone enjoyed themselves on that dance floor!”

- Anna Caudwell

Crafted to recreate the glam of the Roaring 20s, this Art Deco stage display takes audiences through an infinitely evolving architecturally odyssey.

Our client wanted their guests to experience the glamour of the Roaring Twenties, with dancing, music, and lights through the night. To create the illusion, we designed and built a bold, eye-catching Art Deco-style installation, with clean lines and a sunburst fan shape inspired by 1920s-style architectural detailing.

Onto this we projected 3D geometric gold and reflective visuals. These slowly scrolled away into the distance for an infinity tunnel effect, with spontaneous camera flashes adding to the glitzy feel. Complemented by warm lighting and sparkling visuals projected around the venue, the installation formed a perfect backdrop to transport the band and guests back to the Jazz Age.

Tech Used

Custom made Art Deco Style backdrop, 1 x projector, Great Gatsby style ‘Infinity tunnel’ visuals created in-house
