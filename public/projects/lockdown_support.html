Lockdown Support
Andy’s Neighbours

That's lovely Andrew. Hope you're having fun doing this. We love it.”

- Jean Buchanan

1x projector, projection mapping software, custom visual content.

Lockdown in New Zealand was a hard time for many, and we wanted to bring a little bit of light and fun to our community, so we designed and projected these cute little bunny’s, and messages of support on local houses.

The images were randomly timed, with different treats and appearances to keep the kids guessing.
