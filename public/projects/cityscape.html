cityscape
Pasifika Medical Association

Custom made building backdrop and circle projection board. 1 x projector, projection mapping software. Pixel-mapped LED bars.

Tasked with building a skyline of New Zealand’s iconic buildings in just 4 days, we quickly measured up the space and scaled the buildings to fit. Auckland’s Sky Tower, towered at 4.8m tall, with Wellington’s Beehive below standing proud between a mix of other high rises.

Strategically placed LED strip lighting made the buildings pop against the black drape and gave us the ability to change the scene from day, to warm sunset colours, and finally to streetlight glow at night.

One click of a button also transformed the circular projection mapped board from a logo, to the sun, and finally to the moon - tying everything together and smoothly transitioning through the evening.
