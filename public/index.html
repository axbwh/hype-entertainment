Planning an event?
Founded by our design and interaction specialist Andy Binns in 2010, Hype Entertainment was created with a firm belief that good events are an outcome of great design.

Over the past decade, Hype has grown into becoming Wellington’s most innovative and well known multidisciplinary audio visual events company. Accompanied by tech wizard Tom Stonehouse, the Hype team are known for consistently delivering unique & impressive world-class immersive experiences, events, shows and parties.
We provide a range of services to bring our clients' events to life. Our services span event design, prop design & construction, custom signage, projection mapping & interactive installations, lighting, sound, and live streaming services.

We have a wide range of skills and technical equipment, and also work with several trusted event management partners and production suppliers to deliver events of any spec and scale.

Learn More
We have experience working on all types of large scale events from festivals, raves, themed parties & immersive experiences to shows, corporate events, product launches & awards ceremonies. We are known for our innovation, and pride ourselves on delivering experiences beyond expectations.

Learn More
We work with forward thinking clients around NZ and abroad. From well-known tech companies looking to create epic engagements for their staff, event organisers and artists creating captivating public installations, to individuals looking to create memorable events for friends and family, we love to work with people who share a passion for creating experiences.
“We've absolutely loved working with you. We are getting the most incredible feedback on things like "it was like a google event" "it was so spectacular" "the space was transformed".” - Trade Me 2022

Drop us a line
From idea to execution, our tried and tested process has produced exceptional events for hundreds of clients. We’ll begin by discovering your event goals. With fresh ideas in mind, we’ll get into specifics, sketching out a concept that can be achieved within your budget and timeframe.

We’ll coordinate with you and any event partners to ensure the tech is set up and tested before the event starts. With tech support throughout the event, and a quick and seamless packdown afterwards, we’ll make sure it runs smoothly, allowing you to focus on entertaining your guests.

Live Streaming
