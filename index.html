Planning an event?
We work closely with clients to raise the bar and realise fresh new visions. Over the years, we’ve refined our process to ensure every event is more spectacular than the last, and executed without a hitch.
Hitting the process off in person or remotely, we’ll chat about the event, the vision, the vibe. Hash out some ideas, get a feel for what’s possible and make sure we’re all on the same wavelength.
Getting into the specifics, we like to visit the venue and find out everything there is to know about the event, the location, the gear. We’ll sit down and sketch out concepts of what can be achieved within the budget and time-frame.
Once the concepts have been approved, the development phase gets underway. We build, design and code production-ready props, visuals and interactive elements.
We coordinate ahead of time to ensure everything is set-up and packed away efficiently. With the planning out of the way, we can focus entirely on delivering captivating and memorable experiences.
Drop us a line
With all this info, our crew can start iterating on our initial concepts to design riveting visuals and prototype ingenious ways to bring them to life. We ensure everyone is kept in the loop and on board with the art direction.
